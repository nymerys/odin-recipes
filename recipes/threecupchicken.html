Taiwanese Three Cup Chicken Recipe 台灣三杯雞食譜

Description

Taiwanese Three Cup Chicken is a traditional comfort food that is found in many Taiwanese homes.

Spices

Essential spices are the key to distinguishing cuisines from one another. Here are a list of essential spices that will elevate your Three Cup Chicken:

Soy Sauce

This is what creates the saltiness.

Thai Basil

This is the heart of the dish, as its fragrant, minty aroma will elevate the flavor.

Shaoxing Wine

While other wines may be used as substitutes, Shaoxing wine is a staple in Chinese and Taiwanese cooking. Its unique flavor is distinguishable in many Chinese and Taiwanese dishes.

Sesame Oil

Sesame oil is essential in many Asian cuisines. It will add a dark, earthy flavor to this particular dish.

Process

  1. Dice the chicken into small pieces.

  2. Heat a clay pot over high heat and add the sesame oil.

  3. Add the ginger and garlic, and stir-fry until aromatic. Add the chicken and stir quickly a few times.

  4. Add the soy sauce, dark sweet soy sauce, and Shaoxing wine, then continue stir-frying the chicken.

  5. Cover with the lid, lower the heat, and simmer for 5-10 minutes.

  6. Add the basil leaves and stir well with the chicken. Dish out and serve immediately.

Recipe Card

Ingredients

  • 500 grams of chicken drumsticks Substitute: chicken thighs
  • 1 tbsp baking soda
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • 5 cm ginger
  • 6 cloves of peeled garlic
  • 1 1/2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 1/2 tbsp dark sweet soy sauce
  • 1 1/2 tbsp Shaoxing wine
  • 1 bunch Thai basil leaves

Steps

  1. Dice the chicken into small pieces.
  2. Heat a clay pot over high heat and add the sesame oil.
  3. Add the ginger and garlic, and stir-fry until aromatic. Add the chicken and stir quickly a few times.
  4. Add the soy sauce, dark sweet soy sauce, and Shaoxing wine, then continue stir-frying the chicken.
  5. Cover with the lid, lower the heat, and simmer for 5-10 minutes.
  6. Add the basil leaves and stir well with the chicken. Dish out and serve immediately.
